Case Study: Lighting Control

A manufacturer of industrial lighting control products relies on RFM’s WIT modules to control choreographed outdoor lighting at such events as Super Bowls and major 4th of July celebrations. The reason for wireless links is simple: These are temporary setups for which cable runs would be terribly time-consuming and an unnecessary expense.

For extremely large-scale projects, such as light displays on multiple Jacksonville, Florida bridges for Super Bowl XXXIX, the company also used RFM SEMs for long-range communication links with master controls.
