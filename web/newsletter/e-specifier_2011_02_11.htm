Two new additions to Okami™ PoL converter range:

OKL T/1:

 An extension of the OKL series, the OKL-T/1 was designed for applications such as servers, wireless equipment, LED signage, power amplifiers, and other applications across the datacom and industrial markets.
  • Inspectable Land Grid Array (iLGA) format allows easy inspection of solder joints.
  • Maximum 1A output current with a nominal 12V input voltage.
  • Input voltage range, 2.9 to 14 Vdc, wider than the previously released 3A and 6A models.
  • Measures only 12.4 x 12.4 x 4.57 mm (0.5 x 0.5 x 0.2 inches) max.

Data Sheet

OKI T/36:


With a wide input voltage range and a programmable output voltage, the OKI-T/36 series achieves high power conversion efficiency with excellent derating performance. This series is ideal for use in higher voltage applications requiring a low voltage source to power loads such as FPGAs and microprocessors in the datacom and industrial markets.

  • Wide input range of 19-40Vdc.
  • Selectable output voltage between 5.021 and 15.5Vdc.
  • Power output is 36W at 12Vout.
  • Industry standard TO-220 format, with a footprint of just 10.4 x 16.5mm (0.4 x 0.65 inches), ideal for use in space-constrained applications.
