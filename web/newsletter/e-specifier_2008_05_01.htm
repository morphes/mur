BCT Technology

New Distributor in China & Hong Kong

I am excited to announce that we have signed an agreement with BCT Technology (HK) Co, Ltd as our local distributor in China. It’s a great pleasure to give them a warm welcome to the world of Murata Power Solutions.

BCT has been in the power business for very long time, so they can provide a proficient level of technical expertise to our customers in the region. They have a good presence in China with sales offices in Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Shanghai & Beijing and are currently in process to set up more offices across the country.

I am very pleased to add BCT as one of our Distribution partners in the region and confident that they will be able to assist Murata Power Solutions to set a strong foothold and grow the business with our team in China.
