Thursday, March 12, 2009

BSM Technologies appointed as new sales representative

L to R:
Steven Teo (Distribution Account Manager, Murata Power Solutions), V.S. Janardhan (BSM Technologies) and Dan Tan (Regional Sales Manager, Murata Power Solutions)

Murata Power Solutions has appointed BSM Technologies as its sales representative in India. BSM Technologies will sell Murata Power Solutions’ complete range of DC/DC Converters, AC/DC Power Supplies, Magnetics, Data Acquisition devices and Panel Meters.

Established in 1998, BSM Technologies has significant experience and contacts in the power electronics industry.

Commenting on the agreement, Paul Jap, Asia Pacific Sales Director, Murata Power Solutions, said: “We are delighted to be able to welcome BSM Technologies to our sales network and anticipate a very rewarding future for both companies.” .
