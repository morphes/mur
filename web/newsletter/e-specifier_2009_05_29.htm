NEW PRODUCTS: 1200 Series

Shielded inductors provide a power line filtering solution in applications with EMI susceptibility

The new 1200RS and 1200LRS series of inductors are ideal for providing power line filtering in low to medium current applications such as switching power supply circuits. An integral ferrite shield makes them especially useful in designs that are susceptible to electromagnetic interference (EMI).

  • Through hole, radial leaded devices
     
  • 1200LRS series is available with inductance values ranging from 6.8µH to 22mH and DC current ratings up to 4.7A IDC and compact dimensions of Ø10.5x8.5mm
     
  • 1200RS series offers higher current versions with the same footprint. Inductance values extend from 10µH to 1mH and maximum current ratings to 4.6A IDC. Dimensions are Ø10.5x10.5mm
     
  • All parts have a low DC resistance to help maximize efficiency
