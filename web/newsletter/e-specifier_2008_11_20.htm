1W DC/DC converters set new standards for efficiency, miniaturisation and reliability

  • Load regulation performance of <5%

Murata Power Solutions has introduced two new series of isolated 1W, DC/DC converters that set new standards for efficiency, miniaturisation and reliability. The single output MEV and dual output MEA series are the new high performance versions of the company’s well established and popular NMV and NMA series. With efficiency levels from 84%, an improvement of 10% over the existing products, the MEV and MEA series respond to the current industry emphasis on minimising power usage. Improved load regulation performance of <5% makes the new DC/DC converters well suited for use in applications where output voltage variation can not be tolerated. The overall load regulation tolerance envelope has been reduced from 17.5% to 9%.

Why would I specify?

  • Improved efficiency performance across the full load range
  • Full 1W power across industrial temperature range of -40 to 85oC
  • Industry-standard pinout
  • Improved load regulation and voltage set point accuracy.
