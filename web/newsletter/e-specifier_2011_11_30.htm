New compact 3-phase AC digital ammeter
with built-in current transformers


ACM3P Series
       
 
  • Low profile 3-phase AC digital ammeters
  • Suitable for front panel mounting
  • Capable of high precision measurement to within 0.1 A
  • Display of true-rms values of 2- or 3-phase mains current up to a maximum of 100 A per phase
  • Three built-in current transformers
  • Line and phase currents from 480 VAC can be measured, with peak capability of 150 A.

Unlike many digital ammeters available on the market that measure average values, the ACM3P series can accurately display true-rms values of triangle, square and other irregularly shaped AC current waveforms.
