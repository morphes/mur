New 50W isolated DOSA compatible
sixteenth-brick DC/DC converters
offer 4:1 input ranges


UWS Series
 
  • 50W medium power isolated DC/DC converters, available in either through-hole or surface mount.
  • DOSA standard sixteenth-brick dimensions of
    1.31 x 0.91 x 0.36 inches.
  • Compact units that accommodate the wide input range of + 18 to + 75 VDC around a nominal 48 VDC.
  • Input to output isolation is 2,250 VDC so that the output is protected from spikes, transients and noise on the input.
  • Within the series there are three models offering outputs of 3.3, 5 or 12 VDC.
  • Up to 54 W output is available without the need for any forced air cooling.
  • The 12 VDC unit suits regulated intermediate bus applications.
