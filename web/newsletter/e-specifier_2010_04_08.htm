Frequency reading models added to the DATEL range of four-function AC Power Meters

DATEL ACM20 Series

Murata Power Solutions has expanded its offering of Datel Digital Panel Meters with two new additions to its ACM20 series of Four-Function AC Power Meters.

  • New models provide Hertz reading capability plus true-rms Volts, Amps and Watts
  • All ACM20 models have gained UL safety agency approvals
  • Maximum power reading for 100A models increased to 26.4kW
  • Universal 85 to 264VAC operating voltage

Data Sheet

Key Features of the ACM-20 Series:

  • Broadening the range and measurement capability of the ACM20 series makes the AC Power Meters suitable for use in more applications including alternative energy and residential energy monitoring.
  • A front panel selector switch provides two modes of operation: a fixed reading of any of the four parameters, or a continuous cycling through of all four measurements.
  • ACM20 power meters can accurately display the true-rms voltage and current values of triangle waves, square waves, and other irregularly shaped waveforms, with an accuracy of ±1% of full scale.
  • Large (0.36"/9.2mm), bright red LED display, easily readable from as far as 15 feet (5 meters).
