5V input additions to OKAMI™ family of DOSA-compliant PoL DC/DC converters

The latest expansion of Murata Power Solutions' Okami™ non-isolated single output point-of-load (PoL) DC/DC converters sees the introduction of 3A and 5A modules with a 5V nominal input. These new parts complement the 12V input 3A, 5A, 10A and 16A Okami modules recently introduced and give designers an even greater choice of alternatives to discrete design in providing high efficiency Point-of-Load (PoL) power for their embedded applications.
