Regulated DC/DC bus converter combines high efficiency with wide input range for distributed power applications

RBC Series:

Murata Power Solutions has introduced a new cost effective Regulated Bus Converter (RBC) that delivers up to 210W output power, with a Vin range of 36-75V, ultra-high efficiency (92.5% @ full load) with Vout regulation (±1.5%) in a quarter brick open frame package.

  • Optimized for use in distributed power Regulated Intermediate Bus Architectures (RIBAs) where it can be used to drive point-of-load (PoL) converters.
  • Able to accept a wide range 36VDC to 75VDC (48V nominal) input. This is then converted to a 12VDC/17A output.
  • Applications include 48V-powered datacom and telecom installations, base stations, cellular telephone repeaters and embedded systems.
