Okami™ PoL converter range expands with 3 and 6A additions to the OKL series

OKL Series:

3 and 6A models have been added to our Okami™ OKL range of non-isolated point-of-load (PoL) converters, featuring the innovative inspectable Land Grid Array (iLGA) package. The OKL series offers a high power density, high power conversion efficiency (up to 95.3%) solution for space constrained embedded applications and is a cost-effective alternative to discrete design approaches.

  • New input voltage range models offer programmable 3A and 6A outputs
  • iLGA: inspectable LGA interconnect for ease of manufacturing
  • 12.2mm x 12.2mm footprint simplifies inclusion in space constrained embedded designs
  • High power conversion efficiency up to 95.3%
  • Cost-effective alternative to discrete designs
