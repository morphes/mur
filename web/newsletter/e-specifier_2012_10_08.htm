  New Medically Approved 400W
AC-DC Converter Offers High Efficiency and Convection Cooling

 
 
Medical safety certification, UL60601-1 3rd edition

► Industry leading 400 Watts of power packaged in a standard 1U open-frame format measuring just 76.20 x 127.00 x 35.56 mm (3 x 5 x 1.4 inches). The MVAC400 models accommodate the universal AC input voltage range from 90 to 264 VAC with active power factor correction (PFC) and active inrush protection.

These low-profile power converters have significantly better convection-cooled performance characteristics than other competing designs. The MVAC400 can deliver up to 250 W without the need for any forced air flow. This saves cost and reduces power packaging volume requirements. Removing cooling fans also allows designers to increase reliability and reduce system noise.
