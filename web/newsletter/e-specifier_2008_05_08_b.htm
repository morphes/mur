New Distributor:

Codico (Central & Eastern Europe)











L to R: Michael Schrutka (Codico), Sven Krumpel
(Codico), Andy Barber (Distribution Manager,
EMEA), Andreas Hanausek (Distribution Account Manager).

Murata Power Solutions has signed CODICO as our Distributor for Central and Eastern Europe. CODICO offers strong, and importantly, local design in support to their customers.

The well established Austrian company was founded in 1977 and covers Central, and part of Western Europe as well as Eastern European countries from Czech Republic across to the Baltic region, down to former Yugoslavian Countries plus Russia and the Ukraine. Throughout this territory they have a wide network of local subsidiaries. The headquarters offers a variety of logistic value add services to support their customers across the continent and beyond.

This family owned business, located in Vienna, enhances Murata Power Solutions’ strategy by offering technology focus and expertise, short response time, close to the customer – on site support throughout many countries across Europe.  

Andy Barber, Distribution Manager for EMEA, commented on the agreement: ”We are delighted to name another professional Distributor as our partner, to support our endeavour to deliver a good mixture in the availability of our products. CODICO will help Murata Power Solutions to face the challenges in this fast changing industry with close customer contact, technical expertise and logistic services!”
