Murata Power Solutions is participating in Avnet's Power Forum Virtual Conference

September 22nd - 23rd, 2010
8:00 AM to 2:00 PM PST each day

Murata Power Solutions is participating in this Virtual Conference focused on Power. A number of audio presentations will be available to you on demand throughout the day. Select from the list of Power topics which are of interest to you. There will be an online tool for directly emailing the presenters for asking questions, seeking design ideas, etc.

Ron Jungling, FAE for Murata Power Solutions, has prepared a presentation that will assist your design decisions relative to selecting point-of-load DC/DC Converters:

Title: Design Considerations for Point-of-Load (PoL) Power Modules - Murata Power Solutions

Outline: This presentation discusses how to choose and implement a Point of Load (POL) power module for board level designs. Input and output filtering considerations, output voltage programming, use of remote on/off and sequencing output voltages is covered. Current density and price trends are also reviewed. Inspectable Land Grid Array type of SMT connection is shown as a means of improving solderability.

Log-on and view Ron Jungling’s presentation, and all of the topics being presented in the Forum
