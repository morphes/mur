NEW PRODUCT - NMG Series

Murata Power Solutions expands its range of high efficiency DC/DC Converters with introduction of 2W single output NMG series

  • Isolated 2W single output DC/DC Converters.
  • Industry-standard pinout.
  • Typical efficiency up to 88%, 1kVDC isolation.
  • Wide choice of input and output voltages.
  • Full 2W performance across industrial temperature range.

Murata Power Solutions has introduced a new range of isolated 2W single output DC/DC Converters that deliver class-leading performance. The eight models in the NMG series are housed in small footprint (19.5mm x 7.5mm x 10.0mm high), industry-standard SIP packages that allow easy drop-in replacement of other devices. The new DC/DC Converters also provide a pin compatible power upgrade path from Murata Power Solutions’ 1W NMR series.

Why would I specify the NMG?

  • The NMG series is based on established product ranges.
  • Efficiency from 81% at full load.
  • Full 2W power across industrial temperature range of -40 to 85ºC.
  • Improved load regulation and voltage set point accuracy.
