MEA1 Series expands with the introduction of 15V, 24V and 48V input versions

MEA1 Series:

Murata Power Solutions has introduced 3 new input voltages to the MEA1 series in addition to the 5V and 12V input versions released in November 2008. Design-in applications include measurement and control instrumentation, power distribution and switchgear equipment, flow sensors, and PLC's.

  • Full load performance across -40°C to +85°C temperature range
  • 1kVDC galvanic isolation
  • UL 60950 pending
