MEV3, MER1, NMK and NMG now
UL 60950 recognized

We are pleased to announce that following the recent launch of our MEV3 and MER1 series with UL recognition pending, both of these series are now recognized by UL to UL 60950 for functional insulation.

The NMK and NMG are also now UL 60950 recognized.

Applications for these series include instrumentation & control, transportation signaling, factory automation equipment, and power distribution monitoring.
