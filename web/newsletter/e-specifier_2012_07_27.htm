The EMH module provides a complete solution for multi-port Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) applications, including safety certifications. Wide input range accommodates worldwide PoE applications.
The EMH series delivers high efficiency over the input voltage range of 18-72V. The power capability of the module will support four IEEE 802.3 at POE+ ports.
