OKI T/3 and OKR T/6 & T/10

The latest additions to our Okami™ range of PoL DC/DC converters:

OKI T/3 Series:

  • 3A output current
  • Surface mount series
  • Ideal for 24VDC input range applications requiring a low voltage DC source for powering FPGAs and microprocessors
OKR T/6 and T/10 Series:
  • Miniature SIP package
  • Though-hole mounting
  • Combines a wide input voltage range with programmable output voltage
