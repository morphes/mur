NEW DC/DC Product Data Book
Industry’s largest range of DC/DC converters

We are excited to announce our new DC/DC converter databook. This 36-page color catalog provides technical data on our extensive offering of isolated, point-of-load (POL), processor support and memory support DC/DC converters.

The new databook features quick selection guides to help design engineers quickly identify the most suitable DC/DC converter for their given application.
The steps are:

  • Select the product category required
  • Find the output voltage needed from the data tables
  • Choose a suitable product series using the performance and package information shown
  • Download the full data sheet from www.murata-ps.com.

The new catalogue can be requested via the Murata Power Solutions website or downloaded as a PDF at http://www.murata-ps.com/dcdc-catalog
