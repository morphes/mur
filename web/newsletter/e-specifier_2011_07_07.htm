UEE Series
 
  • Fully isolated, open frame DC/DC converters

  • Wide input range from 36 – 75 VDC
  • Highly efficient - typically up to 90%
  • Industry standard DOSA compliant 1/8 brick package
  • Designed specifically for high-volume cost-sensitive telecom, networking, and instrumentation applications
  • Through-hole pin or surface mount option
  • Baseplate option is available for the 25A & 30A through-hole models
