Industry’s first DOSA compliant 1/32 “brick” 30W
DC/DC converter series


ULT Series
 
  • Miniature 1/32 brick
  • DOSA compatible, open frame package
  • Takes up significantly less board space than other 30W products on the market
  • Wide (2:1) input voltage range of 36 – 75 VDC
  • 12Vout, 5Vout, and 3.3Vout models. Lower voltages to come in the near future
  • Maximum output power of 30W is available from the 12Vout model, with the 5V and 3.3V models producing 25W
  • Designed for a wide range of applications including distributed power architectures in Industrial, Telecommunications, Routing, Optical Networking, Wireless equipment and Microwave Radio/point to point communications
