Additions to Okami™ PoL converter range feature ultra-wide input in compact package

OKI-78SR Series: Drop-in replacement for LDOs offering significant efficiency advantages

The new OKI-78SR series of switching regulator converters are ideal for industrial applications. A direct drop-in replacement for industry standard three-terminal 7803 and 7805 series LDOs, the series offers an ultra-wide input voltage range (7 to 36VDC) for nominal 24Vin with either 3.3 or 5V output voltage, depending on the model.

  • Efficiency levels up to 90.5%; a significant advantage over traditional LDO solutions.
  • Small footprint, vertical SIP-mount package.
  • Industry standard TO-220 format, with dimensions of just 10.4 x 16.5mm, ideal for use in space-constrained applications.
