The new C1U-W-1200-12-Tx and C1U-W-1200-48-Tx AC/DC front end power supplies are designed to provide 1200W (110/220VAC) of high efficiency, high density power. They are suitable for use in a wide range of distributed power and redundant (N+1) applications for the delivery of bulk power to servers, workstations and storage systems in sectors such as industrial, automation, test and measurement and control.

The 1U, power factor corrected (PFC) units feature a universal AC input. The C1U-W-1200-12-Tx has a main output of 12V and standby outputs of either 5V or 3.3V; the C1U-W-1200-48-Tx has a main output of 48V and a choice of 5V, 3,3V or 12V standby outputs.

Why would I specify?

  • Both power supplies feature active current sharing on their main output and have a high power density of 9.2W/ cu. inch that helps minimize space required in customer applications.
  • Efficiency levels of 90.6% promote lower cost of operation and reduce the need for thermal management in addition to the cooling fans built into the power supplies.
  • Demonstrated reliability of 6.8Mhrs.
