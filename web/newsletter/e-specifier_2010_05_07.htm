UCE Series expands with the introduction of a 12V output, 10A version

UCE Series:

Murata Power Solutions has introduced a 12Vout @ 10A (120W) 1/8 brick to add to the UCE series of isolated DC/DC converters. Supporting an input voltage range of 36 – 75V the UCE-12/10-D48 provides an isolated output voltage in the industry standard 1/8 brick package.

  • Industry standard eighth-brick pinout and package
     
  • Outputs from 1.5V to 12V up to 120W
     
  • Outstanding thermal performance and derating
