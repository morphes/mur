Integrated design surface mount 1W DC/DC converters bring benefits of IC-style packaging


NTF series 1W surface mount isolated DC/DC Converters are integrated, IC-style true surface mount packages that help simplify their inclusion in customer designs. The wide input range devices are ideal for use in battery powered products plus a wide range of other applications, especially those where high volume is expected.

Comprising nine models with efficiency levels up to 70%, the NTF series is available with a choice of three wide range input voltages of 4V – 6V (nominally 5V), 9V – 15V (nominally 12V) and 18V – 36V (nominally 24V). Tightly regulated output voltages of 5V, 12V or 15V are offered. All devices provide 1kVDC isolation and include a voltage trim feature.

  • Surface mount IC style package with 0.7W/cm3 power density
  • Choice of three wide range input voltages
  • Tightly regulated 5V, 12V or 15V output
  • Voltage trim feature
