NEW PRODUCTS - MPDTH Series
POLA DC/DC Power Converters - 6A, 10A and 15A versions

  • Auto Tracking Feature
  • Choice of modules offering 3.3V, 5V and 12V input voltages
  • Adjustable output voltage of 0.8V to 5.0V
  • High Power conversion efficiency up to 95%
  • Surface-mount and through-hole versions available

Murata Power Solutions has launched its new range of non-isolated, Point-of-Load-Alliance (POLA) DC/DC Converters. The MPDTH series is available in a choice of 6A, 10A and 15A current ratings with nominal input voltages of 3.3VDC, 5VDC or 12VDC for each.

The new modules are ideal for supplying POL power to devices such as ASICs, FPGAs and DSPs in a wide range of multi-processor digital systems in applications including datacoms, industrial and medical equipment.

Why would I specify the MPDTH Series?

  • High power conversion efficiency.
  • Adjustable output voltages can be set by using a single external resistor.
  • Key features include auto-tracking, pre-bias immunity start-up support, on/off control, output trim functions.
  • Protection features include input under-voltage lockout, output short-circuit protection and output over-current and over-temperature protections.
  • With the auto-tracking feature, powering FPGA with two different voltage requirements for core and I/O can be met with relatively simple circuit implementation.



The Point of Load Alliance is a group that promotes multi-sourcing of pin-compatible, non-isolated, point-of-load power modules. It enables power supply designers to choose modules from any member with the assurance that they will provide identical functionality, performance and form factors.
