Murata Power Solutions introduces
4:1 input 204 W Quarter-brick DC/DC converter


UWQ Series
 
  • Isolated open-frame 204 W DC/DC converters
  • Through-hole mount
  • Industry standard quarter-brick format
  • Measuring just 2.3” (58.4) X 1.45” (36.) X 0.46” (11.68)
  • Efficiency 92%
  • UWQra-wide 4:1 input voltage range of 18-75Vdc, ideal for 24V or 48V systems
  • Single output voltage of 12 VDC up to 17A of current
  • Protection features include over current, over temperature, input under voltage and output short circuit
  • Typical applications include: Distributed Power Architectures, Wireless network equipment, Access and Optical Network Equipment, Industrial Equipment
