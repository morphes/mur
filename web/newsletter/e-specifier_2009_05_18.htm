OKL Series: New 12V, 3A iLGA™ SMT Models

We are excited to announce the latest addition to our Okami™ family of industry-standard pad layout, non-isolated point-of-load (PoL) DC/DC converters. The OKL Series are 12V, 3A, iLGA™ SMT modules which provide an ideal alternative for discrete designs that require high density, high efficiency power solutions in a 12.2mm x 12.2 mm area.

What is iLGA™?
An innovative LGA package design that allows easy access of all pin connections for inspection of LGA solder joints.



  • iLGA™ technology allows easy inspection of all the signal pins
  • Output current 3A
  • Programmable output voltage from 0.591-5.5
  • High efficiency up to 93%
  • Wide input voltage range 4.5VDC to 14VDC
  • Optional sequence/tracking feature (OKL2 models): under development
