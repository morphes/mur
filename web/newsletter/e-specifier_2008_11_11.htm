Tuesday, November 11, 2008


Murata Power Solutions signs worldwide distribution agreement with Digi-Key

Murata Power Solutions has entered into a new global distribution agreement with Digi-Key. Under the agreement, Digi-Key will stock products from Murata Power Solutions’ industry-leading ranges of AC/DC power supplies, DC/DC converters, digital panel meters and magnetic devices. Application areas for the products include telecommunications, computing and industrial.

Digi-key is one of the world's fastest growing distributors of electronic components. The company has earned its reputation as an industry leader for its total commitment to service and performance. Murata Power Solutions products will be featured in Digi-Key’s online catalogue and will also be included in future print catalogues. These products will be available to order directly in both prototype and production quantities for rapid delivery to customers across the globe.

Director of Distribution Sales, Murata Power Solutions said: “Murata Power Solutions is pleased and excited to partner with Digi-Key across all global markets. Today’s engineers require leading-edge power products that are available anywhere on the planet in the shortest possible time. Digi-Key’s dominance in the engineering community enhances Murata Power Solutions’ value to the customer in every major market.”

“We are very pleased to expand our power solutions line with products from Murata Power Solutions,” said Mark Larson, Digi-Key president and COO. “I am certain that these quality products will be of consequential interest and appeal to our customers.”
