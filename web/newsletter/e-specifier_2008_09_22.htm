NEW PRODUCTS - CF200 & CF350

Open frame AC/DC Power Supplies are among the industry's smallest 200W & 350W switchers

  • 200W Single Output (12V) and 350W Single Output (12/24/48V).
  • 5V standby output voltage.
  • 1U height.
  • Synchronous Rectification allows high efficiency (>80%).
  • Universal AC input with active PFC.
  • U-frame construction.
  • Worldwide safety and emission standards.
  • Includes internal "ORing" diodes for N+1 operation.

The CF200 and CF350 switching power supplies utilize advanced component and circuit technologies to deliver some of the industry’s smallest 200W & 350W switchers. Both offer universal AC input (85-265VAC) with active power factor correction (PFC) and compliance to worldwide safety and EMC standards.

Why would I specify the CF200/CF350?

  • Proven power topology and components.
  • High power density - up to 9.5W/in3.
  • Compact package and 1U height.
  • Standard features such as ORing diodes and active current share.
  • Murata quality & reliability.
