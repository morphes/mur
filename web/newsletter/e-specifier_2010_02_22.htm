New AC/DC Front End power supply from Murata Power Solutions achieves Climate Savers and 80+ Gold efficiency ratings

D1U4CS-W-2200-12-HA4C:

A new AC/DC front-end power supply for hot-swapping redundant systems that meets Climate Savers and 80+ Gold efficiency ratings.

  • 2200W output power with 12V main output
  • 5V standby voltage
  • 1U sized with 24.5W/cu. in. power density
  • Optional 19in. 1U power shelf provides up to 8.8kW output power

The D1U4CS-W-2200-12-HA4C is designed for delivering reliable, high density bulk power to servers, workstations, storage systems and other 12V distributed power architecture applications.
