NEW PRODUCT ANNOUNCEMENT: D1U 2800 Series

52V, 2800W, 1U Redundant Hotplug AC/DC Power Supply

 Tuesday, January 13, 2009
  • -52V main output with 12V standby
  • 2800W output power
  • 91.6% efficiency at full load
  • Compact 1U (11.5in. x 5.5in x 1.6in.) enclosure with internal cooling fans
  • N+1 redundancy and hot swapping capable
  • I2C bus interface with status indicators

The D1U-H-2800-52-Hx from Murata Power Solutions is a 2800W (220VAC), power-factor-corrected AC/DC front-end power supply that offers industry-leading power density of 27.7W per cubic inch. With 91.6% efficiency at full load (measured after the output Oring diodes and with fans running at full speed), and 92.0% efficiency at 2kW load, the D1U-H-2800-52-Hx also has amongst the best efficiency figures in the industry. The RoHS compliant design is packaged in a 1U low-profile housing and has a main output of -52V and a standby output of +12V.
