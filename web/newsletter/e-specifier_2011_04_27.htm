UEI25 Series extends range of 25W low-profile open-frame DC-DCs to include +3.3 & +5VDC output models

UEI25 Series:

  • 25W output in one square inch of board area
  • Low-profile, measuring just 0.96 x 1.1 x 0.32 inches (24.38 x 27.94 x 8.13mm)
  • 2:1 input voltage range of +36 to +75VDC
  • Output trim function that allows the output to be trimmed up or down by 10% with an external trim resistor
  • Highly efficient operation, up to 91%
  • Surface mount or through-hole packages now available for all models in the series
  • Applications include small instruments, area-limited microcontrollers, data communications equipment, remote sensor systems, telephone equipment, vehicle and portable electronics
