NEW PRODUCT - NMK Series
Isolated 2W single output DC/DC Converter sets new standards in efficiency and load regulation

  • 89% typical efficiency (15V output), 7% output voltage set point accuracy.
  • Maximum load regulation 2.5% (15V output).
  • Wide choice of input and output voltages.
  • 3kVDC isolation and power density to 1.33W/cm3.
  • Full 2W load performance across industrial temperature range.

The NMK series of isolated 2W single output DC/DC converters from Murata Power Solutions provides industry leading performance in a small footprint, pin compatible, industry-standard SIP package that allows easy drop-in replacement of other devices. The NMK series also provides a straightforward power upgrade path from Murata Power Solutions’ existing 1W NMV series.

Why would I specify the NMK?

  • The NMK series is based on established product ranges.
  • Efficiency from 78% at full load.
  • Full 2W power across industrial temperature range of -40 to 85oC.
  • Improved load regulation and voltage set point accuracy.
