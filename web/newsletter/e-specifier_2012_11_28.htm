  Half-Brick DC-DC Converter Delivers Superior Thermal Performance
 
 

► The new PAH series of compact high efficiency medium-power DC-DC converters are capable of delivering up to 450 Watt output. Measuring just 2.3" x 2.4" x 0.5" (58 x 61 x 12.7 mm), the series complies with the DOSA half-brick industry standard footprint. The PAH series mechanical design incorporates a baseplate that provides conduction cooling for demanding thermal environments.

The PAH series was designed to deliver full output power over the entire telecommunications input voltage range of 36 - 75 VDC.

Product Features
•Outstanding thermal performance
•Standard baseplate for conduction cooled applications
•No output reverse conduction
•Input to output isolation, 2250Vdc
•Input under-voltage lockout
•Output over-voltage protection
•Thermal shutdown
•Output short circuit protection
•Certified to UL/EN 60950-1, CSA-C22.2 No. 60950-1
