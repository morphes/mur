NEW PRODUCT: UWE Series

World's First 9-36V (4:1) Ultra Wide Input 1/8-Brick

  • Ultra-wide input of 9VDC to 36VDC or 18VDC to 75VDC
  • Fixed output providing up to 75W of power
  • Pin compatible with quarter-brick modules to allow space saving replacement
  • 1500VDC input to output isolation (basic insulation)

Murata Power Solutions has introduced a new series of isolated DC/DC Converters that are the world’s first modules to offer 4:1 ultra-wide input in an eighth-brick format with 9VDC to 36VDC or 18VDC to 75VDC input. The UWE series provides a fixed output of up to 75W making it ideal as a space saving drop-in replacement for commonly used 100W quarter-brick devices where the full 100W power is not needed.

Why would I specify?

  • The UWE series has a synchronous rectifier design that provides very high efficiency and low power dissipation, allowing the DC/DC Converters to be used without fans or temperature derating in many applications.
  • The UWE series provides a choice of fixed output voltages of between 3.3VDC and 24VDC to ensure compatibility in embedded equipment, CPU cards and instrument sub-systems.
  • An optional thermal mounting baseplate is available to allow operation in the most difficult applications and environments.
  • No reverse conduction allows monotonic start up into pre-biased loads for greatest application flexibility.
