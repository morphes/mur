OKY & OKX Additions:
5 Vin versions of OKX 10/16A SIP & OKY 10/16A SMT

The latest additions to our Okami™ range of PoL DC/DC converters comprise 5Vin versions of both the 10A and 16A models within the OKX and OKY series, all available with negative or positive on/off control. The OKX2/OKY2 variants support the sequence/tracking feature. These modules provide an ideal drop-in replacement for other DOSA-compliant parts in space constrained designs that require high density, high efficiency power solutions.
 
  • Output currents of 10A and 16A
  • Programmable output voltage from 0.7525-3.63
  • Up to 1000µF output capacitance
  • High efficiency up to 95.5%
  • Wide input voltage range 2.25Vdc to 5.5Vdc
  • Optional sequence/tracking feature
