Introduction to ESD Protection Devices

New audio training module
  The increased demand for more features in mobile devices combined with the reduced size of today's ICs makes preventing damage from electrostatic discharge, ESD, even more of a challenge. This tutorial will show how Murata’s LXES Series ceramic and silicon based ESD protection devices address these challenges. ESD protection devices are critical components in protecting sensitive circuits in a variety of electronic equipment, such as mobile phones, notebook PCs, net books, digital cameras and other consumer devices.
