EDLC Technical Guide

DMD/DME Series
  We have developed a Technical Guide for our new DMD/DME series of EDLCs (Supercapacitors), which provides information on structure, electrical principles, and factors for selecting EDLCs.  
 


The guide is located on our Supercapacitor micro-site:
www.murata-northamerica.com/edlc
 

    
 

The Technical Guide provides:

  • Key features and benefits
  • EDLC Structure
  • Equivalent Circuit of EDLC
  • Charge characteristics
  • Discharging Calculations
    
 


Using an innovative design, the EDLCs (Supercapacitors) are extremely thin and low-resistance electrical double-layer capacitors, designed for use as Battery Assist in applications such as Smart Meters and POS, and for high power back-up for SSD (solid state drives) and peak load assist for LED flash.
