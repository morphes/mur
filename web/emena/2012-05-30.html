DMG Series Feature High Temperature Range

 

The new DMG supercapacitors, with an operating temperature range of -30°C to +85°C, are a significant addition to our offering of DME/DMD supercapacitors introduced in October 2011.

The DMG series provide high power back-up, making them an ideal choice for enterprise solid state drive (SSD) manufacturers and telecom last gasp applications.

Features:

  • High voltage with very low ESR
  • Compact package of 20.5mm x 18.5mm x 2.8mm
  • Full operating temperature range from -30°C to +85°C
  • Five-year life span at 70°C operation
  • High capacitance values in small and low profile design
  • Available in 2.1V single cell and 4.2V double-cell:
    • 2.1V model provides nominal capacity of 700mF with a profile of only 1.4mm and 70mΩ ESR at 1kHz.
    • 4.2V model has nominal capacity of 350mF, 2.8mm thickness and 130mΩ ESR at 1kHz.
