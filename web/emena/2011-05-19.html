Murata launches Advanced
Hybrid Crystal Resonator Line

New series leverages package and production technology to to achieve
an innovative balance between accuracy, size, and cost
  The XRCGB Series:
  • Tiny package size of just 2.0 x 1.6 x 0.7mm
  • Low cost
  • Accuracy better than 100ppm
  • Quartz crystal precision
  • Encased in Murata's proven packaging derived from our ceramic resonator technology
  • Four connection pads on the base allowing for use on existing 3225 and 2520 board layouts

 

  
 

 
 
  • Frequencies available: 24, 25, 27, 27.12, 30, 33.86, 40 and 48MHz, with additional frequencies under development.
  • Two versions of the XRCGB series are available: XRCGB-L and XRCGB-M.
  • The XRCGB-L series features initial accuracy of ±100ppm (initial frequency tolerance), the XRCGB-M features two tighter accuracies: ≤30MHz, ±30ppm (initial) and >30MHz, ±45ppm (initial).
