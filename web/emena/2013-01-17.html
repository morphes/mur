  New Ultra-Thin High Peak Current Electrical Double-Layer Capacitors
 
 

► The new ultra-thin DMF series features low resistance (40mΩ to 60mΩ) and stable temperature characteristics, enabling it to be charged and discharged frequently over the operating range of -30°C to +70°C. Initially, the DMF series will offer 330mF and 470mF capacitance values to support various energy requirement demands. Package thickness ranges from 2.5mm to 3.2mm (+/- 0.2mm) and all products in the series provide an instantaneous maximum working voltage of 5.5V.

►The DMF series features the highest power density levels available on the market. The new EDLC design provides an optimized internal configuration to improve energy and power density. In addition, the DMF series offers a simplified, inline terminal configuration that optimizes board space. The low ESR allows for high output current with quick charge and discharge characteristics.


Product Features
•High power output and high energy: 5.5V
•Compact and slim: 21.0mm x 14.0mm x 2.5mm
•Operating temperature range from -30°C to +70°C
•Low ESR: 40mΩ – 60mΩ
•ESR change @ -30°C < twice the nominal ESR
•Low leakage current
•Long cycle life - exceeding 100k cycles
