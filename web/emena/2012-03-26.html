EDLC Tutorial

DMD/DME Series
         
    Electrical Double-Layer Capacitors, also known as Supercapacitors, are essentially energy storage devices offering high power density and a long cycle life. When first introduced, EDLCs were used mostly for low power applications such as memory backup but recent electrochemical and packaging innovations have significantly improved the power capability at the same time as reducing the package size.

View this audio presentation to learn more about Murata’s innovative, thin, low-resistance, electrical double-layer capacitors, the DMD and DME series. This presentation will also cover:

  • Market focus and applications
  • Product features and benefits
  • Technology comparison and basic operation
