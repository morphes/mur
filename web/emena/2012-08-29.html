  Introducing ±50ppm HCR™ (Hybrid Crystal Resonator)
 
 
 

Enabling Murata to expand into new applications in the consumer and industrial markets

► The Murata HCR™ XRCGB series is a 100% crystal timing device. The advantages XRCGB has over conventional crystals are compact size, high reliability, and accuracy at a competitive price. The new XRCGB-F-N series has been added to Murata's HCR™ line-up, which has a frequency tolerance of ±50ppm (initial + temperature), allowing Murata to expand into new applications in the consumer and industrial markets.

Available frequencies for XRCGB-F-N series are: 24, 25, 26, 27, and 30MHz. If the key to a design is accuracy, small package, reliable, robust, and cost effective pricing, then Murata's HCR™ is the number 1 crystal choice!
