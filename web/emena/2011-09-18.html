Murata introduces DMD & DME Series of
Electrical Double-Layer Capacitors

High Power Density
Longer Cycle Life
Ultra-Thin
  Murata Electronics North America today announced the development of thin, low-resistance, electrical double-layer capacitors (EDLCs), often referred to as supercapacitors.

Murata’s DMD and DME series offer a compact part with a high capacitance, high voltage with very low equivalent series resistance (ESR) over a wide temperature range. Additionally, Murata’s EDLCs provide much higher energy storage and power density than any existing conventional capacitor technology. These features make the Murata DMD and DME series ideal for applications requiring burst or pulse loads, like LED flash, audio circuits and power amplifiers. The ability to deliver peak current assistance to the load allows this product to extend battery life between charges.

 

    
 


Specifications:

  • Small, ultra-thin package: 18.5mm x 20.5mm x 1.5mm (single cell)
  • High capacitance value: 700mF – single cell & 350mF – double cell
  • Constant rated voltage 2.1V; rated peak voltage 2.75V, 30mΩ @ 1kHz (single cell)
  • Flexible discharge: 500µAh-2As
  • Operating temperature: -30°C to +70°C
 
Key Features of our EDLCs include:
  • High Power Density
  • Longer cycle life
  • Ultra-thin package
  • High capacitance value
  • Ultra-low ESR

 

  
 

Ideal Solution to:

  • Auxiliary power supply for LED flash units
  • Reduce battery load fluctuations by supporting peak output
  • Control battery load fluctuations from GSM or audio output
  • Backup solution for SSDs or similar devices
  • Support peak output when driving small motors, or in USB powered devices
