New Capacitor Lines for
LED Lighting Applications


New Series Provide Class-Leading DC Smoothing and Acoustic Noise Reduction
  The GR3 and RDE (X7T) lines of multi layer ceramic capacitors (MLCC) were designed specifically to provide unsurpassed DC smoothing and acoustic noise reduction in light-emitting diodes (LED) lighting applications.
 		  
  The GR3 family:
  • Ideal for reflow soldering use
  • Compared to standard ceramic capacitors, there is a 10dB acoustic noise reduction
  • available in 250, 450 or 630 VDC rating
  • Popular capacitances starting from 10nF and extending up to 1,000nF for the 250V, 560nF for the 450V and 270nF for the 630V
  • Package sizes include 0805, 1206, 1210, 1812 and 2220

 

  
 
The RDE series:		  
 
  • Provides radial leaded insertion production capabilities
  • The same voltage rating as the GR3 series with capacitances starting from 33nF to a maximum of 2.2uF for the
    250V, 1.2uF for the 450V and 560nF for the 630V.
 
  With the market for green and eco-friendly products growing by leaps and bounds, LED lighting is
garnering increasing importance. We are committed to achieving the rigorous requirements that
these applications demand. The GR3 and RDE lines are a critical piece in helping designers
ensure the maximum lifetime and energy efficiency requirements of their end
products.
