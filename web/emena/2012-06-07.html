World's Smallest Micro DC-DC Converter

LXDC Series advances mobile device miniaturization and functionality

 

By combining a number of Murata's proprietary materials and technical capabilities, specifically ferrite material expertise, multilayer processing, and power module design, we have developed a product line that delivers ultra-compact size and EMI noise suppression. The LXDC2UR and LXDC3EP series are the world's smallest DC-DC converters, at 600mA and 1A current ratings, respectively, that also incorporate the I/O capacitors into the same package.  Also available is the 3A model, the LXDC55B series.

A key feature of the LXDC line is the embedding of the power inductor within the actual ferrite substrate.  By utilizing this structure, the IC can be mounted directly above the power inductor coil with almost no pattern length to diminish leakage radiation noise.  The I/O connections are also routed through this same ferrite substrate to create a function similar to ferrite beads that significantly reduces conductive noise.

Features:

  • Low EMI noise (power inductor embedded in ferrite substrate)
  • Ultra-compact size up to 10mm2
  • High efficiency
  • 2.3V - 5.5V input voltage range
  • 600mA to 3A maximum load currents (depending on series)
  • Fixed output voltage (1V - 3.3V models)
 

Applications:

  • Discrete dc/dc circuits requiring down-sizing and space savings
  • Solar micro- or nano- inverters
  • Mobile and portable devices
