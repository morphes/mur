Murata Electronics polymer aluminum electrolytic capacitors are ideal for low ESR, high capacitance applications in a variety of commercial and industrial markets. Utilizing innovative design and manufacturing processes, the ECAS series provides a high level of performance, allowing circuit designers to achieve excellent noise suppression, ripple absorption, and output smoothing in power management applications.
