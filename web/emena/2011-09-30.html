When & why to consider Murata’s
Polymer Aluminum Capacitors instead of Tantalum

 
  Supply of tantalum can be scarce and unpredictable, leading to dramatic increases in pricing and lead-times. In contrast, Aluminum, used in Murata’s polymer aluminum ECAS Series, is not subject to the same volatility because it is a more abundant mineral. In addition, polymer aluminum capacitors offer numerous design benefits due to its advanced technology.

This tutorial will provide details on the drawbacks of tantalum and the benefits of using advanced polymer aluminum capacitors from Murata. Specifically, this presentation will cover tantalum supply and pricing concerns, Murata’s tantalum replacement options
and the benefits of Murata’s ECAS Series.
