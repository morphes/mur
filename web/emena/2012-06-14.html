Murata Americas will be exhibiting at IMS 2012

June 19-21 at the Palais des Congrès de Montréal in Montreal, Canada
 

Murata Americas will be exhibiting at the 2012 IEEE International Microwave Symposium (IMS) June 19-21 at the  Palais des Congrès de Montréal  in Montreal, Canada.

Please contact your customers likely to attend and encourage them to visit Murata’s booth. Our Product Specialists and local Rep, GMA, will  be on hand to discuss current applications and future requirements. 

Exhibition Dates/Time:

Tuesday, June 19 -  9:00am to 5:00pm
Wednesday, June 20 - 9:00am to 6:00pm
Thursday, June 21 - 9:00am to 3:00pm
