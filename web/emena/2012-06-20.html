Learn more about our LXDC Series
 

The 600mA rated LXDC2UR and 1A LXDC3EP versions are the world's smallest DC-DC converters, incorporating the I/O capacitors into the same package.  Also available is the 3A model, the LXDC55B.

By combining a number of Murata's proprietary materials and technical capabilities, specifically ferrite material expertise, multilayer processing, and power module design, we have developed a product line that delivers ultra-compact size and EMI noise suppression.
