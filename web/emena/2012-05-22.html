World’s smallest low profile high pressure pump

High pressure micro blower developed through
innovative piezoelectric technology
 

Murata today announced the MZB1001 microblower, a miniature piezoelectric air pump designed for a wide range of household consumer products.  The MZB1001 microblower is believed to be the smallest air pump in the market.  Using a piezoelectric diaphragm, which vibrates up and down when a sine wave voltage is applied and has been optimized for operation above the normal audible range, the vibrations force air into the microblower and out through a nozzle on the top of the device.  Used as an air pump and capable of creating a high air pressure flow, the micro-blower is ideally suited to short duration high-pressure airflow applications.

Features:

  • Small Profile of 20 X 20 X 1.85mm
  • 0.8 liters per minute with 15Vp-p
  • 1.5kPa with 15Vp-p
  • Low power consumption (typical 0.2W)
  • High pressure generation capacity
  • Vibration free

Applications:

  • Air blower for fuel cells
  • Spot cooling of IC’s and electronic circuitry
  • Particulate diffusers using high speed airflow
  • Household fragrance products such as aromatic atomizers and air fresheners.
