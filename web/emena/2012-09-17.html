  Online Wireless Product Tutorial Now Available for Type VK and Type TN Modules
 
 
 


Online training
Follow these training modules to find the quickest and easiest way to integrate Murata modules onto your board. These short and informative presentations provide key product features and highlight the potential advantages to your application. Inside you will find evaluation kit information, and detailed comparisons between the benefits of modules over discrete solutions.


Type VK
The Type VK module is designed to be quickly integrated into microcontroller-based devices, providing 802.11 b/g Wi-Fi™ connectivity. Developed for broad-market, the wireless module offers open documentation from public websites, and is available for purchase in any quantity through distribution partners.

Type TN
The Type TN module is a broad-market solution providing IEEE802.11 b/g/n and Bluetooth v4.0 wireless connectivity. Type TN is part of the broad-market series targeting a wide range of consumer applications.
