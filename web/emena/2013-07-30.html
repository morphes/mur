AUDIO technical presentation: DMF Series Ultra-thin, high peak current electrical double-layer capacitors (EDLC)
 
 
 

►

This audio presentation has been developed to explain why the DMF design and features are ideal for use as an auxiliary power source in LED flash units, digital cameras, cell phones, and peak load assist for GSM PA’s, smart meters, compact motor starters, and USB power bus.

  • Provides the highest power density levels available on the market.
  • Low resistance, 40m to 60m
  • Stable temperature characteristics, enabling it to be charged and discharged frequently over the operating range of -30°C to +70°C
  • Offered with 330mF and 470mF capacitance values to support various energy requirement demands
  • Package thickness ranges from 2.5mm to 3.2mm
  • Provides an instantaneous maximum working voltage of 5.5V
