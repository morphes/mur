New ultra low-profile surface mount 2.4 kHz piezo sounders

 
 

We have expanded our PKLCS family, with the addition of 2.4 kHz SMD piezoelectric sounders, suited for white goods, portable, industrial, and automotive applications.

Both the consumer part PKLCS1212E2400-R1 and the automotive/industrial part PKLCS1212E24A0-R1 are available in low profile surface mount packages:

  • They have a 5dB increase in sound pressure level compared to previous 2KHz surface mount sounder
    • A low frequency operation of 2.4kHz results in pleasing sound that is easier to hear. The small increase in operational frequency, 2.0kHz to 2.4kHz, permits significant gains in loudness, with minimal change in tone.
  • Ideally suited for applications that need to minimize the use of leaded components or have all low profile components.
    • Its thin 3 mm profile make it perfect for use in miniaturized portable electronics.
    • Will survive reflow soldering with no impact to performance.
    • The SMD package enables more efficient manufacturing since the parts can be mounted using pick and place equipment.
 
 

Features:

  • Surface mount , ultra-thin 3mm profile
  • Reflow solderable
  • Loudness increase from previous 2.0kHz model
  • Automotive grade version
  • Low operating frequency (2.4kHz)
  • Power efficient piezo technology

Applications:

  • Automotive
  • Industrial
  • Portable Hand-held Electronics
  • White Goods
